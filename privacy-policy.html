Privacy Policy

Shorts & Reels Blocker

Last updated: July 2025

Overview

Shorts & Reels Blocker is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension is designed with privacy-first principles and collects minimal data necessary for functionality.

Data Collection

We collect NO personal information. The extension operates entirely locally on your device.

What We Store Locally:

  • Extension Settings: Your blocking preferences (YouTube/Instagram toggle states)
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics (optional, stored locally)
  • Performance Data: Error logs for debugging (stored locally only)

Data Usage

The extension uses your stored preferences to:

  • Remember your blocking settings between browser sessions
  • Apply content blocking rules on YouTube and Instagram
  • Provide usage statistics (if enabled)

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any data to third parties.

All data remains on your device and is not sent to external servers.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not integrate with any third-party services or APIs. All functionality is self-contained.

Data Security

Your data is stored securely using Chrome's built-in storage APIs, which provide encryption and access controls.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • View stored data through Chrome's extension management
  • Delete all data by uninstalling the extension
  • Reset settings to defaults at any time

Children's Privacy

This extension does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through the extension update process.
